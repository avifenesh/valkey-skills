## Key design decisions

**Bitnami `bitnami/valkey-cluster` chart** — the Valkey ecosystem's most mature Helm-based operator; handles StatefulSet, cluster-init Job, PDB, anti-affinity, TLS, and metrics exporter out of the box.

**ACL setup is post-deployment** — the Bitnami chart's cluster-init Job authenticates with `AUTH <password>` (default user). Running `ACL SETUSER` on all 6 nodes after the cluster is formed, then issuing `ACL SETUSER default off`, satisfies the "default user disabled" requirement without breaking initialization. The trade-off is that ACL rules are in-memory and reset on pod restart; for persistence, an `aclfile` on a writable PV is the next step.

**TLS** — self-signed CA + server cert with a wildcard SAN covering all 6 pod FQDNs, generated by `openssl` in `deploy.sh` and loaded into a `valkey-tls` Secret.

**`valkey-search`** — loaded via `loadmodule` in `configmap:` Helm values; the `.so` is baked into the custom image at `/opt/bitnami/valkey/lib/valkey_search.so`.
