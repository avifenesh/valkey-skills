### Operator choice: Hyperspike `valkey-operator`

- **Why not the official operator** (`valkey-io/valkey-operator`): explicitly "EARLY DEVELOPMENT — not ready for production", no published container images
- **Why not Bitnami**: requires commercial subscription since August 2025
- **Hyperspike v0.0.61**: published images (cosign-verified), TLS via cert-manager, bundled redis_exporter, cluster mode — most production-capable community operator available

### Image: `valkey/valkey-bundle`

Includes `valkey-search 1.2.0` (GA, March 2026) pre-loaded — no manual `MODULE LOAD` needed.

### ACL strategy

The operator manages `requirepass` (admin password). A post-deploy Job then connects via `CLUSTER NODES` discovery and runs `ACL SETUSER` on every node to create the three users and disable `default`. This is operator-agnostic and works regardless of whether the operator natively supports ACL CRD fields.
