# Governance

Use when you need to understand Valkey's decision-making process, who the maintainers are, or how major changes are approved.

Valkey is governed by a Technical Steering Committee (TSC) composed of maintainers of the main repository. Chair: Madelyn Olson. Organizational diversity rule: no more than 1/3 of TSC members from the same organization.

**Technical major decisions** (new data structures, backward-compat changes, new APIs) require a formal vote with simple majority. A +2 fast-path exists if no negative votes after two weeks.

**Governance major decisions** (adding/removing TSC members, changing governance) require 2/3 supermajority.

Current TSC members and committers are listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`. License: BSD-3-Clause. Governance document: CC-BY-4.0.
